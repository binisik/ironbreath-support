IronBreath - Support

Getting Started

IronBreath is a workout companion for Apple Watch and iPhone with three training modes:

  • Jump Rope - Interval timer with configurable work/rest phases
  • Body Scan - Guided body awareness sessions with heart rate tracking
  • Kieser Training - Log your machine-based workout sets

Requirements

  • iPhone with iOS 17.0 or later
  • Apple Watch with watchOS 10.0 or later
  • HealthKit permission for heart rate and workout tracking

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does IronBreath need HealthKit access?

IronBreath reads your heart rate in real time during workouts and saves completed sessions to Apple Health so they appear in your Activity rings.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. All data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I sync between iPhone and Apple Watch?

Syncing happens automatically via WatchConnectivity when both devices are connected. Workout sessions started on the Watch are synced to the iPhone app.
