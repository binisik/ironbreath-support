IronBreath - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

IronBreath is a workout timer and body scan app for Apple Watch and iPhone. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Access

HealthKit Data: IronBreath reads your heart rate during workouts and saves workout sessions to Apple Health. This data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud Health data. We never access, collect, or transmit your health data to any server.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information is collected
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data is sent to third-party services
  • No account or login required

On-Device Storage

Workout history and settings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. If you use iCloud, your data syncs privately through your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. We have no access to this data.

Crash Reporting

IronBreath uses Sentry for crash reporting. Crash reports may include device type, OS version, and crash stack traces. No personal or health data is included in crash reports.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party advertising, analytics, or social media services.

Children's Privacy

IronBreath does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
