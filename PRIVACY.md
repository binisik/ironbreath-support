# Privacy Policy - IronBreath

**Last updated: March 7, 2026**

## Overview

IronBreath is a fitness and training companion app for iPhone and Apple Watch. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

IronBreath does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

### Data stored locally on your device:
- Training session history (Jump Rope, Body Scan, Kieser Training)
- Heart rate data (read from Apple Watch via HealthKit)
- App preferences and settings

### Data that stays on your devices:
- All training data is stored locally using SwiftData
- iPhone and Apple Watch sync data directly via WatchConnectivity (peer-to-peer, no server)
- iCloud backup/restore uses your personal iCloud account only

## HealthKit

IronBreath requests access to HealthKit to read heart rate data during training sessions. This data is:
- Read only during active sessions
- Stored locally on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers

## Crash Reporting

IronBreath uses Sentry for crash reporting. Crash reports may include:
- Device model and OS version
- Stack traces (no personal data)
- App version information

No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports.

## Third-Party Services

- **Sentry** (crash reporting) - [Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/)

## Data Retention

All local data can be deleted by uninstalling the app. iCloud data can be managed through your device's iCloud settings.

## Children's Privacy

IronBreath does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
